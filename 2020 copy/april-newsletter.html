2020 Season Opens with Two Tournaments

The organization held two golf tournaments in May 2020 after canceling an initial event at Hamilton Elks. New members Donnie Roberts and Scott Shaw participated in these competitions.

Shaker Run Tournament Results

The Saturday tournament at Shaker Run produced the following results:

  • 1st Place: Jerry McIntosh/Jim Drury (129)
  • 2nd Place: Chris Wyler/Jeff Young (131)
  • 3rd Place: Dick Freeman/Steve Conway (137)

Special Awards: "Closest To The Pin" winners included Jim Drury (#2, #6 skins), Roger Campbell (#5), and Russ Yenser (#12).

Low Net Leaders: Herb Holmes (70), Jerry McIntosh (73)

Yankee Trace Tournament Results

Sunday's competition at Yankee Trace provided strong scores:

  • 1st Place: Dennis Daley/Mark Mattingly (151)
  • 2nd Place: Chris Wyler/Jeff Young (152)
  • 3rd Place: Steve Harrison/Jerry McIntosh (155)

Low Net: Dennis Daley and Jeff Young tied at 70.

Hueston Woods Tournament Announcement

The deadline for the Hueston Woods Tournament will be Wednesday June 3, 2020 with a $54 entry fee per player, including greens fees, prizes, and cart rental.
