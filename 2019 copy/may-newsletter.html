Dominant Performances Highlight May Tournaments

The dominant team this season consists of Larry Lawson and Russ Yenser, who have won victories or shared wins across all three tournaments held so far.

Shaker Run Tournament Results (Saturday)

  • 1st Place ($144): Lawson/Yenser - 119
  • 2nd Place ($64): May/Wills - 123
  • 3rd Place: Holmes/Goertemiller - 124

Yankee Trace Tournament Results (Sunday)

  • 1st Place ($70): Simpson/Conway and Yenser/Lawson - 148
  • 3rd Place ($48): Lainhart/McIntosh - 151
  • 4th Place ($40): Wyler/Young - 155

Tournament Recognition

The newsletter welcomes returning member Dave Rager and introduces new members Greg Brogan, David Simpson, and Bob Higgins. A notable achievement: "Bob won 3 closest to the pins over the weekend and David tied for low net."

Scoring Categories

The newsletter includes separate listings for closest-to-pin winners, skins winners, and low net scores across both events.
