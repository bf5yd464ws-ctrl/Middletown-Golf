Volume 23, Issue 3 June 2023 Two-Course Tournament Results

Notable Achievement: Brad Mattingly's Career Low

Brad Mattingly shot his lowest career round, scoring an 82 for 18 holes.

Beavercreek Tournament (Saturday)

The competition featured a two-ball scramble format with 12 teams. First place went to Brad and Mark Mattingly at +62, earning $90. The field was competitive, with scores ranging from +62 down to +8 for twelfth place.

Yankee Trace Tournament (Sunday)

This four-person event had six teams competing with scores clustered tightly together. Only paying 2 places the results were as follows: the first-place team of Scott Shaw and John Stanton alongside Dave Lainhart and Dick Freeman scored 133 for $120.

Upcoming Event & Important Reminder

The group will play at Browns Run on June 17th at approximately noon, with costs remaining at $69 including side games. The format is three-person double best ball, with blind team draws following play.

The organizer emphasizes that golfers must personally input their scores into the GHIN system, noting that handicaps depend on this individual responsibility for accurate tracking.
