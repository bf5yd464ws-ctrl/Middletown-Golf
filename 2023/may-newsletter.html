Volume 23, Issue 2 May 2023 Inaugural Tournament Results

Inaugural Tournament at Heatherwoode

The inaugural tournament featured 20 golfers competing in cool weather conditions. Steve Goertemiller dominated, winning the only skin with a par on hole #13, earning $100.

Tournament Results

Saturday's Placings:

  • 1st Place ($80): Scott Shaw & Russ Yenser at -66
  • 2nd Place ($60): David Simpson & Steve Conway at -67
  • 3rd Place ($40): Herb Holmes & Steve Goertemiller at -68

Low Net Winners:

  • Scott Shaw (-73) earned $57
  • Three golfers tied at -76 for $12

Upcoming Tournaments

Beavercreek and Yankee Trace host events on May 20-21, with entry fees of $59 per day. The format is a 2 man both balls customized stableford on Saturday and a 4 man double best ball on Sunday. Entry deadline: May 10th.

Schedule through October:

  • June 17: Browns Run
  • July 15: Kings Island
  • August 19: Heatherwoode
  • September 16-17: Armco Park & Hickory Woods
  • October 14: Wildwood

Key Administrative Notes: Players must maintain current USGA handicaps and post scores to GHIN. Tournament entries must arrive by Wednesday before the Thursday deadline. Late submissions may result in handicap penalties or tournament exclusion.
