Volume 23, Issue 4 July 2023 New Member Shines at Browns Run

Mike Nuzum's Impressive Debut

The fourth tournament of the year took place at Browns Run with 26 participants. The organization continues to attract approximately 25-28 golfers per tournament and welcomes new members with USGA handicaps. Mike Nuzum, a new member, had an impressive debut. He not only was part of the winning team with Russ Yenser & Dave Lainhart but he also had a closest to the pin and tied for first in low net.

Tournament Results

Top Finish: Yenser, Lainhart, and Nuzum won first place with a team score of 134, earning $135.

Low Net Leaders (tied at 74):

  • Russ Yenser
  • Mike Nuzum
  • Dick Freeman
  • Ryan Crist

Upcoming Event & Administrative Reminders

Kings Island Golf Course will host the next tournament on Saturday, July 15th at approximately 12:30 p.m. The entry fee is $59 per person (excluding side games), using a four-man triple best ball format with blind team draws. The deadline for registration is July 5th.

An important reminder was issued that the August 19th Heatherwoode tournament requires entries 14 days in advance. Additionally, the league plans to audit member scores to ensure proper entry into the USGA handicap system.
