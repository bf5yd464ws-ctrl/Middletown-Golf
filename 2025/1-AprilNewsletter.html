April 2025

If anyone knows of any new golfers who would like to play in MAGA, have them contact Russ Yenser at 513-571-7931. Membership is open to all golfers.

Below is the 2025 schedule and formats. Prizes will be awarded for accumulated totals for Money Winners, Net Points, and Side Games. All tournaments count in year-end totals. The last 3 tourneys will be double points. Any tournament with fewer than 21 golfers will be counted as double net points.

You may send in your yearly application fee and first tournament fee whenever you want. Because of rising golf costs, entry fees will increase slightly this year. The application fee is $25. Mail entries to:

Russ Yenser
503 Cranewood Drive
Trenton, Ohio 45067
