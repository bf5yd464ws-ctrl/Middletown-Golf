August 2025

The 5th tournament of the year was played at Kings Island on an extremely wet day. The tournament was cut down to a nine-hole event because of the rain. The course issued a $27.50 nine-hole credit to all participants. Payouts were divided in half.

Kings Island Results (July)

1st $40 — 28: Steve Conway & David Simpson
2nd $16 — 31: Roger Campbell & Russ Yenser; Mark Mattingly & Dave Lainhart; Mark Willenbrink & Don Hartness

Next: August 16 — Heatherwoode

Format changed to 3-man double best ball. Sign up as a foursome; blind draw for 3-man teams after golf. Entry fee $68. Entries must be received 14 days in advance — due by August 2nd.

Advance Notice — September

September 13 at Cassel Hills (replacing Armco Park). Tee times at 1:00. Format: 2-man best ball. Entry fee $60. No cookout this year.
