Middletown Area Golf Association
📅 April 2025 · Vol. 25

If anyone knows of any new golfers who would like to play in the Middletown Area Golf Association, have them contact me for more information. Russ Yenser — 513-571-7931. Membership is open to all golfers.

Below is a list of the 2025 schedule and formats. Again this year prizes will be awarded for the accumulated totals for Money Winners, Net Points and Side Games. All tournaments will count in tabulating the year end totals. The last 3 tourneys will be double points. Any tournament with less than 21 golfers will be counted as double net points.

Information for this year and past years can be obtained by going to our website at www.tjcope.net/maga.

Important Notes
Entry Fees: Because of the rising cost of golf, entry fees will increase a little this year to make up the difference. Entry fee does not include side games. The application fee will increase to $25 this year.
Mailing Address Update: We are doing away with the Post Office Box. All entries that are to be mailed should now be sent to:

Russ Yenser
503 Cranewood Drive
Trenton, Ohio 45067
Note: Shaker Run is still up in the air. Nothing has been confirmed for September 14, 2025. You may send in your yearly application fee and first tournament fee whenever you want. Applications are available on the website.
