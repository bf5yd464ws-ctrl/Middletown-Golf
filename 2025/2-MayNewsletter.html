May 2025

The first tournament of the year was a lucky weather day — we avoided the rain. Other than a little wind and some bumpy greens it turned out okay.

A reminder: in MAGA we are to putt all strokes until the ball reaches the bottom of the hole. No gimme putts, and no picking up and taking a number on a hole. Because we award net points you must finish the hole or take an X.

The deadline for entry is 10 days prior to the date of play. Get entries in the mail early — allow at least 7–10 days. Or drop off at: Russ Yenser, 503 Cranewood Drive, Trenton OH 45067.
