September Tournaments at Armco Park and Hickory Woods

September tournaments were held at Armco Park and Hickory Woods. The organizer notes, "It would be nice if we could increase those numbers to about 32 per tournament next year." A winter golf trip is being planned for mid-March 2023, pending participant interest and the organizer's recovery from shoulder surgery.
