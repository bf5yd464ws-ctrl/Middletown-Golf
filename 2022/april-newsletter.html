2022 Season Information and Schedule

The 2022 Middletown Area Golf Association season has started with no changes from last year. The organization would like to welcome all of our new golfers and remind them that entry deadlines are crucial given our expanded membership distribution.

During the offseason, the organization lost two longtime members: Jerry McIntosh passed away in December 2021 and Larry Lawson in January 2022. Barry Wills has also departed after relocating to Florida. New golfers interested in joining are encouraged to contact Russ Yenser at 513-571-7931.

Year-end prizes will be awarded in three categories: Money Winners, Net Points, and Side Games. The final three tournaments will offer double points. A post-tournament celebration is planned for September at Armco Park.

Updated applications are required for all members, including name, email, GHIN number, course location, and phone. For more information, visit WWW.TJCOPE.NET/MAGA
