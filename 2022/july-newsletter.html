4th Annual Tournament at Browns Run

The 4th annual tournament took place at Browns Run with 26 participants. Consistent attendance of 25-28 golfers demonstrates strong member engagement. Both new members and returning veterans are encouraged to join, as there is a lot more money to be won than on Saturday weekly golfing.

Member Health Updates

The newsletter includes updates on several members' medical situations. Don Hartness is recovering from hip replacement surgery, Jim Drury had a partial knee replacement, and other golfers are managing ongoing health challenges.
