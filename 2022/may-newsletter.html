Hamilton Elks Tournament Results

The first tournament of the 2022 season featured 30 players at Hamilton Elks, including six newcomers: Ryan Crist, Brad Mattingly, Garrison Ryan, Stu Locke, Tim Greive, and Jason Harrison. The organization acknowledged donations made after the deaths of longtime members Jerry McIntosh and Larry Lawson, with over $700 collected for the Lawson family during the pandemic.
