2023 Season Welcome

The 2023 season features several updates from the previous year. Members are encouraged to participate in tournaments, though attendance is optional. The organization emphasizes timely entry submissions and plans a post-tournament social event in September at Armco Park with grilled food.

Membership Information

Membership is open to all golfers. Contact Russ Yenser at 513-571-7931 for new member information.

New and returning members must submit updated applications including name, email, GHIN number, handicap location, phone number, and birthday.

Tournament Schedule

The season runs April through October across nine events at various Ohio golf courses. Tournaments employ multiple formats including best ball variations and Stableford scoring. The final three tournaments offer double points toward year-end standings.

Participants sign up either individually or in groups depending on the event, with partner draws occurring either before or after play. Handicaps factor into all competitions, and prizes are awarded for money winnings, net points, and side games.
