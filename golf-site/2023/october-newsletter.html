September Tournament Results

September tournaments took place at Armco Park and Hickory Woods golf courses. The newsletter notes that participation could be improved, requesting members to increase those numbers to about 32 per tournament next year.

Saturday Results (Armco Park)

Gross Scoring:

  • 1st Place ($80): Russ Yenser & Chris Wyler at -52
  • 2nd Place ($60): Don Hartness & Roger Campbell at -53
  • 3rd Place ($40): Steve Conway & John Stanton at -54

Net Scoring & Special Categories: Low net winners, skin winners, and closest-to-pin competitors were announced across multiple holes.

Sunday Results (Hickory Woods)

Gross Scoring:

  • 1st Place ($80): John Stanton & Scott Shaw at -61
  • 2nd Place ($60): Mark Willenbrink & Don Hartness at -63
  • 3rd Place ($40): Russ Yenser & Dick Freeman at -65

Year-End Standings

With one tournament remaining, Mark Willenbrink leads net points with 178, followed by Russ Yenser (176) and Ryan Crist (153). Dave Lainhart leads money winnings at $225, with Scott Shaw dominating side money at $174.

Final Tournament

The final tournament is scheduled for October 14, 2023 at Wildwood, featuring two-man single best ball format. The entry deadline is October 4, 2023.
