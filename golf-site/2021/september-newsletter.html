Volume 21, Issue 6 September 2021 Armco Park & Hickory Woods Results

September Two-Day Tournament

This newsletter covers Middletown Area Golf Association golf tournament results from September 2021, held at Armco Park and Hickory Woods courses. The organization has grown to approximately 28 golfers throughout the year, with organizers hoping to reach 32 participants per tournament in the future. Several players underwent season-ending surgeries, and the planned winter golf trip in March 2022 was cancelled.
