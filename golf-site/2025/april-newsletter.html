Welcome to the 2025 Season

We are excited to launch the 2025 season of the Middletown Area Golf Association. Thank you to all members for your continued participation and support.

The membership fee for 2025 has been increased to $25 to help offset rising operational costs. Tournament entry fees have also been adjusted slightly due to escalating golf course prices.

Important: Entry fees do NOT include side games. Side games (closest to pin, skins, etc.) are additional and optional.

Scoring System

Members compete in three categories:

  • Money Winners
  • Net Points
  • Side Games

Final three tournaments count double toward season standings. Tournaments with fewer than 21 golfers count double net points.
