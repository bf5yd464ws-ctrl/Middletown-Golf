May Tournaments and Scoring Reminder

Armco Park has been cancelled for 2025. The facility management gave their golf outing booking to another group. We are working to identify a replacement venue for September. Additionally, there will be no cookout this year.

Please remember to mark incomplete holes as “X” on the scorecard. Incorrect scores affect net points calculations for all members and can skew the season standings. Take time to verify your scores before submitting.
