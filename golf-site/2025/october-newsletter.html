September Results and Final Season Update

This has been the smallest tournament attendance in the association's history. Several events fell well below our typical participation levels, with the smallest tournament drawing only 8 participants. The executive committee is concerned about declining interest and is evaluating the future of the association. If you have thoughts, suggestions, or commitments regarding participation for the 2026 season, please reach out to Russ Yenser at 513-571-7931.
