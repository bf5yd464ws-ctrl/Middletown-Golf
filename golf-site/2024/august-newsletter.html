Volume 24, Issue 5 August 2024 Kings Island Tournament Results and Upcoming Events

Kings Island Tournament Crowns Team Champions

The July tournament at Kings Island Golf Course featured three-person team competition with blind team draws. First place went to Russ Yenser, Brad Mattingly, and Don Hartness with a score of 128.

Low Net Highlights

Russ Yenser led the low net competition with a score of 69 ($58), followed by Dick Freeman at 71 ($28) and David Saniga at 73 ($9).

Heatherwoode and Armco Park Upcoming

Heatherwoode - August 17th: Entry fee: $65 plus side games. Format involves "sign up as a twosome and blind draw for 4 man triple best ball." Entries required 14 days in advance.

Armco Park - September 14th: Entry: $50. Two-man best ball format with half handicap. Post-tournament meal provided.
