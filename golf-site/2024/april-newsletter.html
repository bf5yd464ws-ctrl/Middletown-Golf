Volume 24, Issue 1 April 2024 2024 Season Launches with New Tournament Formats

Welcome to the 2024 Season

The 2024 season introduces several modifications to the Middletown Area Golf Association tournament schedule and format. Members need not participate in every event, though consistent participation helps with annual standings. The organization aims to recruit new members while retaining existing ones.

New golfers interested in joining should contact Russ Yenser at 513-571-7931. The organization welcomes golfers of all skill levels.

Updated Membership Requirements

All members, including returning ones, must submit updated applications listing: name, email, GHIN number, home course, phone, and birthday. The application fee has increased to $25 this year. Entry fees are also rising slightly due to increased golf costs.

Tournament Format Changes

Several courses are introducing new competition formats this season. Armco Park is introducing a new competition format, Wildwood is using a pre-round draw system, and all tournaments utilize handicap scoring. Heatherwoode requires registration 15 days before the event.

2024 Tournament Schedule

Eight tournaments are scheduled from April through October at various Ohio courses, with formats ranging from 2-man to 4-man competitions and varying point structures for year-end standings.

A post-tournament grilling is planned in September following the Armco Park event. Individual sign-ups are required for two of the final three tournaments.
