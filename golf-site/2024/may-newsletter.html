Inaugural Tournament Features 23 Golfers

The inaugural tournament featured 23 golfers competing in windy conditions on recently aerated greens. Tournament organizer Russ Yenser noted the challenging course conditions while emphasizing administrative requirements for all participants.

Entry Deadlines and Requirements

Entry deadlines are set 10 days before tournament dates. Russ Yenser provided his mailing address for physical entries and stressed that "entries sometimes take 7 to 10 days to get to the post office," recommending early submission. Participants must maintain current USGA handicaps in the GHIN System. "If you don't have a paid USGA handicap... by next tournament your handicap will be lowered," creating scoring penalties for non-compliance.

GHIN Handicap Verification

Members must post tournament scores in their GHIN handicaps, with random verification checks implemented to ensure compliance and integrity of tournament standings.
