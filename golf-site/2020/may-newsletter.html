Hueston Woods Draws Record Attendance

The third tournament of the year took place at Hueston Woods with 32 participants, representing the highest attendance in several years. The organizer expressed optimism about growing membership and noted that tournament winnings exceed typical weekly Saturday play.

Tournament Winners at Hueston Woods

  • 1st Place: Jeff Young & Chris Wyler ($100)
  • 2nd Place: Russ Yenser & Larry Lawson ($80)
  • 3rd Place: Dennis Daley & Mark Mattingly ($60)

Health Update

The newsletter coordinator disclosed a health challenge, stating: "I have been diagnosed with follicular lymphoma cancer." The individual noted the condition is treatable and manageable with proper medical intervention, expressing confidence in maintaining a normal lifestyle if treatment proceeds as expected.

Upcoming Tournament

The next tournament is scheduled for Sunday, July 19th at Kings Island Golf Course at approximately 12:30 PM. Entry cost is $54 per person, with a four-man triple best ball format. The registration deadline is July 9th.
