Volume 20, Issue 8 December 2020 2020 Season Review

2020 Season Conclusion

The 2020 golf season concludes with successful tournaments and strong member participation despite the COVID-19 pandemic adjustments and challenges faced by the organization leadership.

Looking Ahead to 2021

Tournament season will run April through October. A Florida golf trip in mid-March involves approximately 16 participants, with costs around $1,150 for six rounds. The organization remains committed to growing membership and maintaining the traditions of the Middletown Area Golf Association.

Website Resources

Members can access tournament information and member resources at TJCOPE.NET/MAGA. The website provides comprehensive tournament schedules, results, and standings throughout the season.
