USGA Handicap System Changes

The USGA implemented handicap system changes in 2020. The organizer emphasized that "golfers can take a score of more than two strokes over par minus their handicap on that hole," affecting how scores are recorded. The newsletter stressed proper GHIN score submission, warning that incomplete entries may disadvantage other participants in blind draw events.

2021 Plans

Tournament season will run April through October. A Florida golf trip in mid-March involves approximately 16 participants, with costs around $1,150 for six rounds.
