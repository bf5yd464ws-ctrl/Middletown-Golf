Beavercreek Golf Course Tournament Results

The fifth tournament of the season took place at Beavercreek under favorable conditions. The winning team achieved a score of 127.

Top Finishers

  • 1st Place: Larry Lawson, Don Hartness, Roger Campbell (127) - $120
  • 2nd Place: Jim Drury, Rich Mealey, Mark Willenbrink (133) - $90
  • 3rd Place: Bob Higgins, Dave Lainhart, Jim Greer (134) - $60

Skins Winners

Mark Mattingly won on hole 1, Dave Simpson on hole 8, Jim Greer on hole 9, Scott Shaw on hole 10, and Roger Campbell on hole 18.

Year-to-Date Money Leaders

  • Jeff Young leads with $160
  • Roger Campbell ($135)
  • Dennis Daley ($130)

September Tournaments Scheduled

Two tournaments are scheduled for September:

  • Saturday, September 12th at Armco Park with "two man best ball with players receiving half of their handicap"
  • Sunday, September 13th at Walden Ponds

Both tournaments carry $35-$55 entry fees. A Wildwood tournament follows on October 10, 2020. Registration closes Wednesday, September 2, 2020.

Net Leaders Year-to-Date

Mark Willenbrink tops the standings with 112 points.
