Middletown Area Golf Association · Est. 2001
Volume 19, Issue 7 December 2019 2019 Season Review

2019 Season Concludes Successfully

The 2019 golf season concludes with strong member participation and memorable tournaments throughout the year. The Middletown Area Golf Association continues to grow and build community among its members.

2019 Season Highlights

The organization averaged about 24 golfers per tournament and expressed interest in growing to 32 participants for the 2020 season. The season featured tournaments at premier golf courses including Shaker Run, Yankee Trace, Hueston Woods, Kings Island, Beavercreek, Armco Park, Walden Ponds, and Wildwood.

Tournament Winners and Leaders

Strong competition throughout the season produced multiple winners and leaders. David Simpson, Dave Lainhart, and Mark Willenbrink led the money and net points standings heading into the final tournament.

Looking Ahead to 2020

A February 2020 golf trip is being organized with accommodations and six days of competition for approximately $1,000 per person. The newsletter notes that "Last year we paid out more than $3000 in prize money," demonstrating the association's commitment to rewarding member participation and competitive play.

Member Resources

Member TJ Cope continues to maintain the informational website at TJCOPE.NET/MAGA providing members with comprehensive resources and tournament information throughout the year.
