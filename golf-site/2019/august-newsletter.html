Volume 19, Issue 5 August 2019 Kings Island Results

Kings Island Tournament Under Challenging Conditions

The fifth tournament of the year took place at Kings Island under pleasant weather conditions, with a brief rain interruption during play.

Gross Score Winners

  • 1st Place: Rich Mealey & Steve Harrison (55) - $100
  • 2nd Place: Don Hartness & Mark Willenbrink; Steve Conway & David Simpson (59) - $70
  • 4th Place: Jeff Kinsel & Carl Kohs (61) - $40

Low Net Leaders

  • Rich Mealey: 59 ($72)
  • Steve Goertemiller: 66 ($36)
  • Mark Willenbrink and Don Hartness: 67 ($6 each)

Year-to-Date Standings

  • Money Winnings Leaders: Steve Conway: $148; David Simpson: $148; Russ Yenser: $121
  • Net Points Leaders: Mark Willenbrink: 110; Larry Lawson: 97; Jim Drury: 91
  • Side Money Leaders: Jim Drury: $150; Dennis Daley: $148; Rich Mealey: $128

Website Launch

Member TJ Cope created an informational website at TJCOPE.NET/MAGA to distribute member information.

Upcoming Tournament

The next tournament was rescheduled from Saturday, August 17th to Sunday, August 18th at Beavercreek Golf Course at the course's request to accommodate a previously rained-out outing. This schedule change resulted in a reduced fee structure: $24 for golf plus a $40 entry fee (total $50 with side games).
