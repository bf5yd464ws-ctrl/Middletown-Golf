Volume 19, Issue 1 April 2019 Season Kickoff

2019 Season Opens at Hamilton Elks

The first tournament featured strong participation with 28 players. Winners included:

Tournament Winners

  • 1st Place ($100): Russ Yenser and Larry Lawson at 64
  • 2nd Place ($60): Two teams tied at 65 (Wills/May and Simpson/Conway)
  • 4th Place ($40): Hartness/Willenbrink at 66

Low Net Winners

Low net winners ranged from Dennis Daley (71) earning $66 down to Mark Willenbrink (73) winning $11.

Important Administrative Reminder

The newsletter emphasizes: "For the people who have not paid for their USGA handicap that needs to be done or you will be penalized." Members were warned that missing GHIN System registration by the next tournament would result in handicap penalties.

Upcoming Tournaments

Tournaments are scheduled through October at various courses:

  • May 18-19: Shaker Run (Saturday, $50) and Yankee Trace (Sunday, $55)

Entry deadline: May 8th. Tee times begin around 8:30 both days. Members must submit entries by Monday for processing by Thursday.
