Middletown Area Golf Association · Est. 2001
Volume 19, Issue 6 October 2019 Season Nears Conclusion

Final Tournament at Wildwood Scheduled

The group averaged about 24 golfers per tournament and expressed interest in growing to 32 participants for the following year. Armco Park and Walden Ponds hosted September events, with the final tournament scheduled for Wildwood on October 12, 2019.

  • Low Net Winner: Jeff Young at 54
  • Closest to Pin winners and Skin winners listed across multiple holes

Sunday Results at Walden Ponds

  • Low Net Winner: Steve Harrison at 62

Year-End Leaders (with one tournament remaining)

  • Money Winnings Top 3: David Simpson: $234; Dave Lainhart: $201; Mark Willenbrink: $187
  • Net Points Leader: Mark Willenbrink (210 points)
  • Side Money Leader: Dennis Daley ($202)

Winter Golf Trip Planning

A February 2020 golf trip is being organized with accommodations and six days of competition for approximately $1,000 per person. The newsletter notes that "Last year we paid out more than $3000 in prize money."
