2012 Season Overview

The 2012 Middletown Area Golf Association season featured 10 tournaments played across the region, with events at premium courses including Weatherwax, Beckett Ridge, Hueston Woods, Heatherwoode, Kings Island, Beavercreek, and Wildwood. Members competed in various formats ranging from 2-man best ball to 4-man triple best ball and stableford scoring.
