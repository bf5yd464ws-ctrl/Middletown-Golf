Welcome to the Association

The Middletown Area Golf Association provides a tournament format at least once a month throughout the season, played at some of the best courses in the greater Cincinnati area. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or just looking for friendly competitive golf, the Association has a spot for you.

This site provides up-to-date information on tournament events, tee times, results, and newsletters. Entries must be received 10 days prior to each tournament date.
