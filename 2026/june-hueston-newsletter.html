From the Commissioner

The 3rd tournament of the year was played at Hueston Woods on a overcast but comfortable day. The course was in good shape except for the sand traps. Congratulations for a low round of 77 shot by Mark Willenbrink. Right behind him was Mark Mattingly with a 79.

New Website and Shaker Run Makeup

New Website is: Middletowngolf.com

Do you want to make up Shaker Run rainout maybe on August 23rd after the Heatherwoode tourney on Sunday? Give me some feedback to see if there is enough interest. That would be a $148 entry for both days not including side games.
