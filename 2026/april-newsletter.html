From the Commissioner

The first tournament of the year was a nasty & cold weather day. We were able to get the round in but it was not ideal for golf. I thought the greens were fast but smooth.

In MAGA we are to putt all strokes until the ball reaches the bottom of the hole. Also, no one is to pick up and take a number because you are hacking on the hole. Because we are given net points you must finish the hole or take an X and not a score for just handicap purposes.

This year we will exchange scorecards to make sure both parties agree on their scores. There was one mistake the last tournament in the adding of someone’s score.

Make sure you post your MAGA scores in your GHIN handicap after our tournaments. I will randomly check to see if scores were entered.

Entry Deadlines & Contact

The deadline for entry into tournaments is 10 days prior to the date of play. If mailing entries, it sometimes takes 7 to 10 days to reach us. So get them in early.

For the people who have trouble getting their entry in on time, you need to make sure it gets in the mail ahead of time to be received by Wednesday so I can get the entries before Thursday. Because of slow mail, remember to mail your entries early — allow at least 7 to 10 days before the due date.

If you don’t want to mail, entries can be dropped off at:

Russ Yenser
503 Cranewood Drive
Trenton, Ohio 45067

If anyone knows of other golfers who would like to play in MAGA, have them get in touch with me.
