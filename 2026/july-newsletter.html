From the Commissioner

The 4th tournament of the year was played at Kings Island on a shaky weather day. Our next tournament will be August 22nd at Heatherwoode. The tournament was mostly cart path only and seemed to be slow. Maybe it was because the sixsome that played in front of us was the problem. The new pro shop and restaurant was geared for tennis and not golf. Trash was flowing out of every trash can and the cart barn is way too far from the pro shop.

Up Next: August 22 — Heatherwoode

Entries for Heatherwoode must be paid 14 DAYS IN ADVANCE. Entry fee is $69 plus side games. The format is a two man single best ball. You can sign up with your own foursome.
