From the Commissioner

Let’s start with the latest news for Middletown Area Golf Association. Starting with the next tournament, TJ Cope will no longer be sending out and updating the website. I would like to thank him for all the work and time he put into the website. He has not played since 2015 but has continued to help me in posting all the stats for Middletown Area Golf Association.

The new website will be Middletowngolf.com — it is a new look and all info can be seen on this site.

The weekend was not a total washout. Even though Saturday’s golf was only good for about 4 holes, Sunday was beautiful and nice.

The 2nd tournament of the year was played at Yankee Trace.

Shaker Run — Rain-Out Notice

For those of you who got rained out at Shaker, I have given you $10 and put you in the Hueston Woods tournament. If you do not play that tournament we will credit you to another tournament or give back your entry fee.
