September Tournaments Complete with Chris Wyler Dominating Both Weekends

The September tournaments took place at Armco Park and Hickory Woods. Chris Wyler dominated both weekend events, claiming first place and low net honors on both days. The organization hopes to "increase those numbers to about 32 per tournament next year."

Final Tournament at Wildwood

The final tournament of 2024 is scheduled for October 12 at Wildwood Golf Course. Entry costs $65, with entry deadline of October 2. Format: two-man single best ball with noon tee times.

Year-End Competition

Season leaders are emerging across multiple categories. David Simpson and Brad Mattingly lead the money winnings. Dick Freeman and Steve Goertemiller pace the net points standings.
