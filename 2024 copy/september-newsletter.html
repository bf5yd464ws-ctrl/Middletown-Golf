Heatherwoode Tournament Showcases Brad Mattingly's Personal Best

Brad Mattingly achieved a personal best with a score of 75 at the August Heatherwoode tournament. The tournament featured four-person teams drawn blind from two-person sign-ups, using triple best ball format with 4-man teams.

Low Net Winner

Brad Mattingly won low net honors with a score of 58.

September Schedule

Two tournaments are scheduled: Armco Park on September 14th at 8:45 AM (two-man shamble format, $50 entry) and Hickory Woods on September 15th at noon ($65 entry, deadline September 4th).

Season Money Leaders (Mid-Point)

Brad Mattingly and David Simpson are tied at the top in money leaders. Brad Mattingly also leads in other competitive categories, showcasing strong performance through the first half of the season.
