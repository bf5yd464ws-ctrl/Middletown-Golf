Final Wildwood Tournament Concludes Competitive 2024 Season

The final 2024 tournament at Wildwood attracted 14 participants, described as disappointing compared to most tournaments that year which drew 20-28 golfers. Leadership expressed concerns about membership engagement during the season.

Membership Engagement Concerns

Leadership expressed concerns about membership engagement, noting: "There have been many golfers who have expressed a desire not to play with certain golfers because of slow play." A new policy effective 2025 institutes a $25 forfeiture for members who cancel after paying entry fees, with exceptions for medical emergencies or deaths.

2025 Season Outlook

Tournament fees remain $65 (except Armco Park). Entry deadlines are 10 days advance notice, 14 days for Heatherwoode. Proposed dates span April through October at established local courses. The organization aims to improve participation and maintain competitive integrity.

Thank You and Looking Forward

The Middletown Area Golf Association thanks all 2024 participants for their engagement and support. The 2024 season featured eight tournaments across Ohio's finest courses and strengthened the competitive spirit of our membership. We look forward to an even more successful 2025 season with improved participation and continued competitive excellence.
