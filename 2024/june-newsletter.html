Tournament Integrity Emphasized

The organizer addressed several issues affecting tournament integrity. Participants must maintain pace with preceding groups and avoid delays between nines. Additionally, golfers who don't complete holes should record an "X" rather than guessing scores, as inaccurate submissions affect overall tournament standings.

GHIN Record Keeping

Members must personally input scores into their GHIN accounts. The organizer maintains separate Middletown Area Golf Association handicap records calculated from tournament results.

Walden Ponds Upcoming

Walden Ponds is scheduled for June 15th using a "2 man customized stapleford" format. Entry deadline is June 5th, with $75 entry fees and tee times around noon.
