Volume 24, Issue 4 July 2024 Walden Ponds Tournament and Ball Identification Reminder

Walden Ponds Tournament Draws 20 Participants

The fourth tournament of the year at Walden Ponds attracted 20 participants. The organization encourages "new players to join" and notes they must possess a USGA handicap rating.

Ball Identification Reminder

Tournament organizers address a procedural concern: one member played an incorrect ball twice during the same round at Walden Ponds. The newsletter suggests "he gets a different color ball, like pink, so he will know which is his."

Kings Island Coming July 20th

Next tournament scheduled for Kings Island Golf Course on July 20th at 12:30 PM. Format: three-person double best ball with blind team draws. Entry deadline: July 10th. Cost: $65 per person.
