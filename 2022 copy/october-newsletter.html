2022 Season Championship at Wildwood

The final 2022 golf tournament season was held at Wildwood with 20 participants. Almost all of our tournaments were played with about 24 to 28 golfers. The organization expressed interest in strategies to boost participation moving forward.

Notable Achievement

Ryan Crist achieved the "triple crown" as a first-year member, winning in all three season championship categories.

2023 Season and Future Plans

The 2023 season is scheduled to run April through October. A Florida trip is tentatively scheduled for mid-March pending sufficient interest (minimum 12 golfers).

The organization reminds members that "not putting in all your scores may be cheating partners in the blind draw."
