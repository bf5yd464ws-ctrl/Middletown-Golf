Volume 21, Issue 2 May 2021 First Tournament Results

2021 Season Opens with Strong Participation

The first tournament of 2021 featured 28 players at Hamilton Elks, including two newcomers Mark Nagle and Dave Saniga. Dave Saniga claimed victory in his debut tournament, winning both team and low net competitions. Terry Halderman also returned after a six-year absence.

Hamilton Elks Tournament Results

Place Players Score
1st Jeff Kinsel/Carl Kohs and Donnie Roberts/Dave Saniga 63
3rd Don Hartness/Mark Willenbrink 67
4th Scott Shaw/Terry Halderman 66

Low Net Winner: Dave Saniga (69)

Administrative Reminders

Players were advised about USGA handicap requirements. For the people who have not paid for their USGA handicap that needs to be done or you will be penalized.
