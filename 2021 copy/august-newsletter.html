Volume 21, Issue 5 August 2021 Beavercreek Tournament Results

Beavercreek Tournament

The August tournament was held at Beavercreek Golf Course. The course was in great condition and the weather was pleasantly hot. September 18th and 19th tournaments are scheduled for the upcoming month.

Tournament Results

Place Players Score Payout
1st Lawson/Yenser and Mattingly/Daley 126 $140
2nd Conway/Simpson and Saniga/Roberts 130 $80
3rd-7th Various teams 133-136

Special Competitions

Skins Competition ($20 per hole): Notable winners included Jerry McIntosh (holes #1, #17), Roger Campbell (#2), and Jim Greer (#7)

Closest to Pin Contests ($20 each): Distributed across holes #6, #8, #13, and #17

Low Net Scores: Dennis Daley and Jim Drury both carded 71 ($58 prize), Richard May shot 73 ($13 prize)

Season Leaderboards

Money Leaders, Net Leaders, and Side Money Leaders lists provided top 10-11 performers, with Roger Campbell and Jerry McIntosh ranking highest across categories.
