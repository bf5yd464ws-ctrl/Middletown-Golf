Volume 20, Issue 3 June 2020 Kings Island Coming

Kings Island Tournament Scheduled for July

The next tournament is scheduled for Sunday, July 19th at Kings Island Golf Course at approximately 12:30 PM. Entry cost is $54 per person, with a four-man triple best ball format. The registration deadline is July 9th.

Handicap System Requirements

Members are reminded that all golf scores must be recorded in the handicap system, not solely Middletown Area Golf Association tournament rounds. In order for the handicap system to work all scores should be recorded not just tournament scores.

Website Launch

TJ Cope has developed a member information portal at TJCOPE.NET/MAGA providing members with resources and tournament information.

Upcoming Tournament at Beavercreek

After Kings Island, the next event is scheduled at Beavercreek Golf Course with an entry fee of $55 per player. The format involves a three man blind draw after golf for teams using a double best ball structure.
