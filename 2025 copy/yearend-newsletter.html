Final Tournament Results and 2025 Season Summary

The 2025 season saw significant challenges with declining participation. Average tournament attendance ranged from 16–20 golfers, with the smallest event drawing only 8 participants. This represents a concerning trend that the executive committee is addressing. The future of the association depends on renewed member commitment and participation. We are actively seeking feedback from members about their interest in continuing with the association in 2026.

2026 Policy Changes

Based on 2025 experiences and member feedback, we have implemented the following policy changes for the 2026 season:

PolicyChange
Withdrawal Policy Players who withdraw after paying the entry fee will forfeit $20 toward the prize fund (except in cases of medical emergency or death).
Scoring Integrity Scorecard exchange has been implemented to prevent scoring discrepancies and ensure accurate net points calculations.
Hole Completion All holes must be completed. Violation results in disqualification from the event.
Entry Fees Entry fees will increase due to rising golf course costs. The season continues April through October.

Thank You
