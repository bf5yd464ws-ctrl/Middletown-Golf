Upcoming Tournament at Kings Island

The next tournament is scheduled for Sunday, July 21st at Kings Island Golf Course at approximately 12:30 p.m. Format: Two-person best ball. Entry cost: $54. Deadline: July 11th.

Seasonal Dominance

The dominant team this season consists of Larry Lawson and Russ Yenser, who have won victories or shared wins across all three tournaments held so far.
