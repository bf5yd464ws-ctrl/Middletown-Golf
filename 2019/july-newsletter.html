Volume 19, Issue 4 July 2019 Hueston Woods Record Attendance

Hueston Woods Draws Record Participation

The fourth tournament of 2019 took place at Hueston Woods with 31 participants, marking the highest attendance in four years. "This is the most players we have had in 4 years," the newsletter states, expressing hope for continued growth in tournament participation.

Team Competition Winners

  • 1st Place: Steve Conway & David Simpson (score 57) - $96
  • 2nd Place: Jim Drury & Jerry McIntosh (score 58) - $72
  • 3rd Place: Don Hartness & Mark Willenbrink (score 50) - $64
  • 4th Place: Herb Holmes & Steve Goertemiller; Roger Campbell & Jim Greer (score 49) - $44

Low Net Leaders

  • Jim Drury: 67 (-$90)
  • Jim Greer: 68 (-$45)
  • Seven golfers tied at 71

Jim Drury's Outstanding Performance

Jim Drury earned the tournament overall, winning "low net, finished 2nd in team, 2 skins and 1 closest to pin totaling $206."

Season Standings

  • Money Leaders: Larry Lawson & Russ Yenser: $121 each; Steve Conway & David Simpson: $113 each
  • Net Points Leaders: Mark Willenbrink: 86 points; Larry Lawson: 83 points; Jerry McIntosh: 82 points

Upcoming Tournament at Kings Island

Kings Island Golf Course hosts the next tournament on Sunday, July 21st at approximately 12:30 p.m. Format: Two-person best ball. Entry cost: $54. Deadline: July 11th.
